About Us

Trusted Construction Experts Since 2005

Founded by Mr. Bachu Singh, Neelmani Construction has been a cornerstone of quality construction in the region for nearly two decades. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry.

With a team of skilled professionals and a portfolio of successful projects, we take pride in transforming our clients' dreams into reality. From residential homes to commercial complexes, we approach each project with the same level of dedication and craftsmanship.

Our philosophy is simple: quality construction, transparent processes, and lasting relationships with our clients.
